Who we are

Academicfeed was founded in June 2015 by six brilliant students from SCE - The College of engineering.Those students tired of emailing files to themselves and backup academic files in many different places, and share it with other students without order.

Our mission

Academicfeed founded in hope to improve their main goal:Provide to any department in SCE new platform for manage academic materials, share exams and lessons and send messages between students in the most comfortable way in one place.Today, many students across SCE use academicfeed to always have their stuff at hand, share with others and work on team projects.
